    Herald¶

    Advanced Discord Bot for Hunter: The Reckoning 5th Edition

    Herald is a comprehensive character management and dice rolling bot designed specifically for Hunter: The Reckoning 5th Edition gameplay. Beyond basic dice rolling, Herald provides advanced character tracking, H5E-specific mechanics, and quality-of-life tools to streamline your supernatural hunting experience.

    üèπ Why Herald?¶

    Herald was built from the ground up for Hunter: The Reckoning 5th Edition. Unlike generic dice bots, Herald understands H5E mechanics:

    • H5E-Native: Designed specifically for Hunter, not adapted from other systems
    • Character Integration: Roll using your character's actual stats
    • Edge & Desperation: Full implementation of H5E's unique mechanics
    • Beginner Friendly: Clear commands, helpful errors, and in-Discord help
    • Always Improving: Regular updates based on community feedback

    üéØ Example: Quick Roll¶

    /roll_char character:Raven attribute:Dexterity skill:Firearms difficulty:2

    Herald automatically: - Looks up your character "Raven" - Gets their Dexterity and Firearms ratings - Rolls the combined pool - Checks for successes vs. difficulty 2 - Displays results with Edge/Desperation if applicable
