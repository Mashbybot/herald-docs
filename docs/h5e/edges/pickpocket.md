# Pickpocket ü§è

Sleight of hand. Misdirection. What's yours is mine.

Pickpocket represents supernatural skill at theft, misdirection, and sleight of hand. You can steal anything from anyone, plant evidence undetected, and manipulate objects with impossible precision. Nothing is safe from your fingers.

---

## Overview

### What is Pickpocket?

Pickpocket is an **Aptitude Edge** that grants superhuman dexterity and misdirection for theft, planting items, and manipulation. When you invoke Pickpocket, your hands move faster than eyes can track, and your misdirection is supernaturally effective.

The Edge operates through **supernatural dexterity**: fingers that move impossibly fast, attention manipulation that makes people ignore your actions, and precision that defies human limits.

### Who Should Take This Edge?

**Ideal For:**
- **Infiltrators** - Steal keys, plant bugs
- **Thieves** - Professional criminals turned Hunter
- **Investigators** - Plant evidence, steal intel
- **Redeemers** - Non-violent conflict resolution
- **High Dexterity builds** - Maximize manual precision

**Consider Carefully If:**
- Your chronicle doesn't involve theft/infiltration
- You prefer direct approaches
- You have low Dexterity
- Storyteller restricts theft mechanics

### Key Strengths

- **Perfect theft** - Steal anything undetected
- **Evidence planting** - Frame enemies or create alibis
- **Infiltration** - Steal keys, badges, codes
- **Combat utility** - Disarm, steal weapons
- **Non-lethal** - Resolve conflicts without killing

### Limitations

- **Situational** - Not useful in all scenarios
- **Non-combat** - Doesn't help in fights directly
- **Requires targets** - Need people/objects to manipulate
- **Storyteller permission** - What you can steal varies
- **Moral complications** - Theft has consequences

## All Perks

### Supernatural Dexterity

**What It Does:**
Hands move supernaturally fast and precise. Add your Desperation rating to all sleight of hand, pickpocketing, and manual dexterity rolls.

**Mechanical Effect:**
- All theft rolls: Base pool + Desperation
- Applies to: Pickpocketing, lock picking, sleight of hand, palming objects
- Supernatural speed and precision

**When To Use:**
- **Theft** - Steal wallets, phones, keys
- **Planting** - Place bugs, evidence, tracking devices
- **Lock picking** - Open locks supernaturally fast
- **Sleight of hand** - Magic trick-level manipulation

**Strategy Notes:**
- Foundation for all Pickpocket perks
- Higher Desperation = better theft
- Pairs with Larceny skill
- Essential for infiltration builds

**Example:**
```
Need to steal keycard from security guard
Roll: Dexterity 4 + Larceny 3 = 7 dice
Supernatural Dexterity + Desperation 3: 7 + 3 = 10 dice
/roll pool:10 difficulty:3 comment:Stealing keycard
Success: Keycard stolen, guard doesn't notice
Access secured
```

**Herald Commands:**
```
/edge edge:Pickpocket
/perks edge:Pickpocket perk:Supernatural Dexterity
```

### Crowd Work

**What It Does:**
Steal from multiple targets simultaneously. In crowds, you can pickpocket several people in single action.

**Mechanical Effect:**
- Normal: One theft per action
- Crowd Work: Margin of success = number of thefts
- Requires crowd/multiple targets

**When To Use:**
- **Mass theft** - Steal from many people
- **Resource gathering** - Wallets, phones, credentials
- **Chaos creation** - Mass pickpocketing causes confusion
- **Information gathering** - Steal multiple phones/devices

**Strategy Notes:**
- Best in urban/crowded environments
- Pairs with Streetwise (urban knowledge)
- Creates diversions and confusion
- Resource acquisition

**Example:**
```
Crowded nightclub, need resources
Crowd Work: Move through crowd stealing
Roll: 8 successes, Difficulty 2, Margin 6
Steal from 6 people: 3 wallets, 2 phones, 1 keycard
Resources acquired, no one notices
```

**Herald Commands:**
```
/perks edge:Pickpocket perk:Crowd Work
```

### Plant Evidence

**What It Does:**
Place objects on targets undetected. Frame enemies, plant tracking devices, or deliver items secretly.

**Applications:**
- **Framing** - Plant incriminating evidence
- **Surveillance** - Place GPS trackers, bugs
- **Delivery** - Give items without detection
- **Sabotage** - Plant devices on enemies

**When To Use:**
- **Frame enemies** - Plant evidence of crimes
- **Track targets** - GPS on vampire's car/person
- **Infiltration** - Plant bugs in enemy base
- **Reverse theft** - Give items back secretly

**Strategy Notes:**
- Reverse pickpocketing
- Requires items to plant
- Very useful for investigators
- Can backfire if discovered

**Example:**
```
Need to track vampire to lair
Plant Evidence: Place GPS tracker on vampire's jacket
Roll: Dexterity 4 + Larceny 3 + Desperation 3 = 10 dice
Success: Tracker planted undetected
Follow vampire to lair location
```

**Herald Commands:**
```
/perks edge:Pickpocket perk:Plant Evidence
```

### Combat Disarm

**What It Does:**
Steal opponent's weapon during combat as an action. Disarm enemies supernaturally fast.

**Mechanical Effect:**
- Combat action: Attempt disarm
- Roll: Dexterity + Larceny vs target's defense
- Success: Weapon stolen, you hold it
- Can use stolen weapon immediately

**When To Use:**
- **Weapon denial** - Remove enemy firepower
- **Vampire disarm** - Steal their weapon
- **Capture over kill** - Non-lethal takedown
- **Weapon acquisition** - Take their gun

**Strategy Notes:**
- Non-lethal combat option
- Very useful against armed enemies
- Can arm yourself from enemies
- Pairs with unarmed combat

**Example:**
```
Vampire attacking with sword
Combat Disarm: Attempt to steal sword mid-swing
Roll: Dex 4 + Larceny 3 + Desperation 3 = 10 dice vs vampire's defense
Success: Sword stolen from vampire's hands
Next turn: Attack vampire with their own sword
```

**Herald Commands:**
```
/perks edge:Pickpocket perk:Combat Disarm
```

### Misdirection

**What It Does:**
Create supernatural distraction, allowing allies to act unnoticed. Draw all attention to you while teammates operate freely.

**Mechanical Effect:**
- Action: Create distraction
- Effect: All enemies/observers focus on you
- Allies gain bonus to stealth/sneaking
- Duration: One round

**When To Use:**
- **Team infiltration** - You distract, allies sneak
- **Combat positioning** - Allies flank while enemies watch you
- **Escape** - Distract pursuers for ally escape
- **Theft coordination** - You distract, ally steals

**Strategy Notes:**
- Team-focused ability
- Sacrifice your stealth for ally advantage
- Great for coordinated operations
- Pairs with performance/deception skills

**Example:**
```
Need ally to reach control room unseen
Misdirection: You create massive distraction
- Perform elaborate "magic trick" for guards
- All attention on you
- Ally sneaks past with supernatural stealth bonus
- Mission accomplished through teamwork
```

**Herald Commands:**
```
/perks edge:Pickpocket perk:Misdirection
```

## Strategy Guide

### Early Game (Characters 0-20 XP)

**Starting with Pickpocket:**
- **Focus:** Basic theft and infiltration
- **Best Perks:** Supernatural Dexterity, Plant Evidence
- **Build:** Dexterity 4, Larceny 3-4, Stealth 3
- **Tactics:** Steal keys, plant trackers, non-lethal approaches

**Recommended Build:**
- Dexterity 4, Wits 3, Composure 3
- Larceny 4, Stealth 3, Subterfuge 2
- Awareness 2 (spot opportunities)
- Supernatural Dexterity first

**Infiltration Pattern:**
- Scout target location
- Identify key holders
- Steal credentials
- Plant surveillance
- Report to team

### Mid Game (Characters 21-50 XP)

**Developing Pickpocket:**
- **Add Perks:** Crowd Work, Combat Disarm
- **Expand Role:** Infiltrator + support thief
- **Build skills:** Raise Larceny and Stealth
- **Team coordination:** Work with investigators

**Advanced Tactics:**
- Mass theft for resources (Crowd Work)
- Combat support through disarms
- Comprehensive surveillance placement
- Non-lethal enemy neutralization

**Progression:**
- Raise Larceny to 5 (maximum theft skill)
- Raise Dexterity to 5 (maximum precision)
- Stealth to 4-5 (infiltration)
- Second Edge: Senses (spot theft opportunities), Fleet (escape artist)

### Late Game (Characters 51+ XP)

**Mastering Pickpocket:**
- **All Perks:** Master thief and infiltrator
- **Perfect Theft:** Nothing is safe
- **Combat Control:** Disarm anyone
- **Team Support:** Misdirection and coordination

**End-game Tactics:**
- Steal anything from anyone undetected
- Plant comprehensive surveillance networks
- Combat disarm to neutralize threats non-lethally
- Misdirection for team operations

**Ultimate Build:**
- Dexterity 5, Wits 4, Composure 3
- Larceny 5, Stealth 5, Subterfuge 4
- All Pickpocket perks
- Second Edge: Senses (perfect infiltrator)
- Untouchable thief

### Edge Synergies

**Works Great With:**
- **Redeemer Creed** - Non-lethal approaches
- **Innocent Creed** - Reluctant thief using skills for good
- **Senses Edge** - Spot opportunities, see threats
- **Fleet Edge** - Steal and escape impossibly fast
- **High Dexterity** - Maximum precision

**Conflicts With:**
- **Direct combat builds** - Pickpocket is subtle
- **Honor-focused concepts** - Theft conflicts with honor codes
- **Low Dexterity** - Can't utilize effectively

**Neutral:**
- **Arsenal, Library, Inspire** - Different focus but compatible

### Using Pickpocket in Play

Pickpocket operates through stealth and precision:

1. **Identify target** - What do you want to steal/plant?
2. **Position** - Get close to target
3. **Roll theft** - Dexterity + Larceny + Desperation
4. **Success** - Object stolen/planted undetected
5. **Consequences** - What do you do with it?

**Example:**
```
Need vampire's phone for intelligence
Supernatural Dexterity active
Approach vampire at nightclub
/roll pool:10 difficulty:3 comment:Pickpocketing phone (Dex 4 + Larceny 3 + Desperation 3)
Result: 6 successes, margin 3
Phone stolen perfectly, vampire doesn't notice
Extract intelligence from phone
```
