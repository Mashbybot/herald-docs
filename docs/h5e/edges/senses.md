## Overview

### What is Senses?

Senses is an **Aptitude Edge** that grants superhuman perception across all sensory modalities. When you invoke Senses, you perceive details impossible for normal humans - see in total darkness, hear heartbeats, smell fear, and sense supernatural presences.

The Edge operates through **supernatural perception**: your senses transcend human limits. You perceive reality more completely than others, noticing details, threats, and opportunities that are invisible to normal people.

### Who Should Take This Edge?

**Ideal For:**
- **Scouts** - Reconnaissance and early warning
- **Investigators** - Notice all clues
- **Defenders** - Detect threats early
- **Hunters** - Track any prey
- **High Wits builds** - Maximize perception

**Consider Carefully If:**
- You have low Awareness skill
- Your chronicle doesn't reward perception
- You prefer direct action over observation
- Sensory overload would bother you thematically

### Key Strengths

- **Perfect perception** - Notice everything
- **Threat detection** - Never surprised
- **Investigation** - Find all clues
- **Tracking** - Follow any trail
- **Supernatural sight** - See what others can't

### Limitations

- **Information overload** - Too much input can overwhelm
- **Doesn't grant action** - See threats but still must respond
- **Sensory attacks** - Heightened senses = vulnerability to loud/bright attacks
- **Requires interpretation** - See clues but must understand them
- **Desperation cost** - Using Senses increases Desperation

## All Perks

### Enhanced Perception

**What It Does:**
Add your Desperation rating to all Awareness and perception rolls. See, hear, and sense beyond human limits.

**Mechanical Effect:**
- All perception rolls: Base pool + Desperation
- Applies to: Awareness, Investigation (finding clues), Survival (tracking)
- Supernatural sensory enhancement

**When To Use:**
- **Spotting threats** - Notice ambushes
- **Finding clues** - Discover hidden evidence
- **Tracking** - Follow trails
- **Surveillance** - Observe targets
- **Investigation** - Perceive all details

**Strategy Notes:**
- Foundation for all Senses perks
- Higher Desperation = better perception
- Pairs with Awareness skill
- Essential for scouts and investigators

**Example:**
```
Investigating vampire crime scene
Enhanced Perception active
Roll: Wits 4 + Awareness 3 + Desperation 3 = 10 dice
/roll pool:10 difficulty:2 comment:Finding clues
Result: 7 successes, margin 5
Notice: Blood splatter pattern, scent of vampire, hidden camera, victim's phone under couch, supernatural residue
See everything others miss
```

**Herald Commands:**
```
/edge edge:Senses
/perks edge:Senses perk:Enhanced Perception
```

### Darkvision

**What It Does:**
See perfectly in complete darkness. No penalties for low light, total darkness, or nighttime operations.

**Mechanical Effect:**
- No darkness penalties
- See in total darkness as if daylight
- Color vision in darkness (supernatural)

**When To Use:**
- **Night operations** - Fight in darkness
- **Underground** - Navigate caves, sewers
- **Stealth approach** - Operate without lights
- **Darkness advantage** - You see, enemies don't

**Strategy Notes:**
- Major tactical advantage
- Pairs with Stealth (approach in darkness)
- Essential for night-focused Hunters
- Negates common environmental penalty

**Example:**
```
Vampire lair in abandoned subway tunnels (pitch black)
Normal: -3 dice penalty for darkness
Darkvision: No penalty, see perfectly
You see vampires sleeping in darkness
Ambush them with full vision advantage
```

**Herald Commands:**
```
/perks edge:Senses perk:Darkvision
```

### Danger Sense

**What It Does:**
Supernatural warning of imminent threats. Perceive danger before it manifests, gaining advantage on avoiding ambushes and surprise attacks.

**Mechanical Effect:**
- Cannot be surprised
- Advantage on initiative when danger is near
- Intuitive warning of threats
- May act before ambushers

**When To Use:**
- **Ambush situations** - Detect ambush before it triggers
- **Trap detection** - Sense danger from traps
- **Assassination attempts** - Feel danger before attack
- **Supernatural threats** - Sense supernatural predators

**Strategy Notes:**
- Prevents surprise
- Keeps you alive in dangerous situations
- Pairs with Lightning Reflexes (Fleet) for always acting first
- Narrative and mechanical benefit

**Example:**
```
Walking into vampire ambush
Danger Sense triggers: "You feel wrongness, hair stands up"
Roll initiative before ambush resolves
You act first despite being ambushed
Turn ambush on ambushers

### Predator's Nose

**What It Does:**
Track targets by scent like a supernatural predator. Follow scent trails, identify individuals by smell, and detect invisible/hidden enemies by scent.

**Capabilities:**
- **Track by scent** - Follow hours-old trails
- **Identify by smell** - Recognize individuals
- **Detect hidden** - Smell invisible/hidden enemies
- **Emotional scent** - Smell fear, aggression, deception

**When To Use:**
- **Tracking** - Follow fleeing enemies
- **Detection** - Find hidden/invisible foes
- **Investigation** - Scent evidence at scenes
- **Identification** - Recognize supernatural creatures by smell

**Strategy Notes:**
- Counters invisibility/stealth
- Essential for tracking
- Supernatural creatures have distinctive scents
- Very effective against vampires (smell blood)

**Example:**
```
Vampire fled after fight, need to find lair
Predator's Nose: Follow scent trail
Trail leads through city for miles
Track to lair location
Smell other vampires inside (count: 3)
Perfect intelligence through scent

## Strategy Guide

### Early Game (Characters 0-20 XP)

**Starting with Senses:**
- **Focus:** Basic enhanced perception
- **Best Perks:** Enhanced Perception, Darkvision
- **Build:** Wits 4, Awareness 4, Investigation 3
- **Tactics:** Scout ahead, spot threats early

**Recommended Build:**
- Wits 4, Intelligence 3, Dexterity 3
- Awareness 4, Investigation 3, Survival 2
- Firearms 3 (respond to threats you detect)
- Enhanced Perception first, Darkvision second

**Scout Pattern:**
- Scout ahead of team
- Enhanced Perception to spot everything
- Darkvision for night/dark operations
- Report threats to team
- Engage from prepared positions

### Mid Game (Characters 21-50 XP)

**Developing Senses:**
- **Add Perks:** Danger Sense, Predator's Nose
- **Expand Role:** Scout + tracker + early warning
- **Build skills:** Raise Awareness and Investigation
- **Team integration:** Intelligence hub

**Advanced Tactics:**
- Never surprised (Danger Sense)
- Track any target (Predator's Nose)
- Comprehensive perception (Enhanced + Darkvision)
- Provide perfect reconnaissance

**Progression:**
- Raise Awareness to 5 (maximum perception)
- Raise Investigation to 5 (understand what you see)
- Raise Occult to 4 (interpret supernatural)
- Second Edge: Fleet (perfect scout - see everything, move fast)

### Late Game (Characters 51+ XP)

**Mastering Senses:**
- **All Perks:** Perceive everything, including supernatural
- **Supernatural Sight:** See the invisible
- **Perfect awareness:** Nothing escapes notice
- **Ultimate scout:** Team's eyes and ears

**End-game Tactics:**
- Supernatural Sight for complete awareness
- Danger Sense prevents all surprises
- Predator's Nose tracks anything
- Enhanced Perception + Darkvision = perfect perception
- Omniscient scout role

**Ultimate Build:**
- Wits 5, Intelligence 4, Dexterity 4
- Awareness 5, Investigation 5, Occult 4
- All Senses perks
- Second Edge: Fleet (ultimate scout - perceive everything, move impossibly fast)
- Supernatural intelligence gatherer

### Edge Synergies

**Works Great With:**
- **Defender Creed** - Detect threats to protect others
- **Innocent Creed** - Notice everything while learning
- **Fleet Edge** - Perfect scout (perception + speed)
- **Library Edge** - Notice clues, research findings
- **Scrounger Edge** - See hidden resources
- **High Wits** - Process perceptions faster

**Conflicts With:**
- **Low Awareness** - Can't use effectively
- **Action-focused builds** - Perception without action
- **Oblivious concepts** - Thematic clash

**Neutral:**
- **Arsenal, Status, Inspire** - Different focus but compatible

### Using Senses in Play

Senses operates through enhanced perception:

1. **Automatic perception** - Storyteller describes what you perceive
2. **Active searching** - Roll Awareness + Desperation
3. **Specialized senses** - Apply relevant perks (Darkvision, Predator's Nose, etc.)
4. **Interpret findings** - Use Intelligence/Occult to understand

**Example:**
```
Entering vampire haven
Enhanced Perception active (always)
Storyteller: "Your enhanced senses immediately detect:"
- Darkvision: See perfectly in dim lighting
- Supernatural Sight: Three vampire auras, two human auras (victims?)
- Predator's Nose: Smell blood (fresh), death scent, vampire scent (hours old)
- Danger Sense: No immediate ambush, but presence of powerful vampire (elder)
- Enhanced Perception roll: /roll pool:10 difficulty:2 comment:Perceiving details
Result: Notice hidden cameras, escape routes, coffins in basement, recent struggle signs
Complete tactical picture from perception
Plan assault with full intelligence
```
