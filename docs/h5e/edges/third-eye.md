## Overview

### What is Third Eye?

Third Eye is an **Endowment Edge** that grants supernatural perception - the ability to see, hear, and sense things beyond normal human capability. You perceive the occult world directly: ghosts walking among the living, auras revealing emotions and nature, magic crackling in the air, spirits invisible to others.

The Edge operates through **mystic sight**: your perception extends beyond the physical into the supernatural. You are never blind to the hidden world.

### Who Should Take This Edge?

**Ideal For:**
- **Supernatural investigators** - See evidence others miss
- **Spirit mediums** - Communicate with ghosts
- **Mystics** - Natural supernatural sensitivity
- **Occult specialists** - Understand what you see
- **Paranormal researchers** - Study the invisible

**Consider Carefully If:**
- Your chronicle is low-supernatural (nothing to see)
- You prefer combat focus over investigation
- Storyteller doesn't use hidden supernatural elements
- You want offensive capabilities (Third Eye is perception-only)

### Key Strengths

- **See the invisible** - Ghosts, spirits, incorporeal entities
- **Aura reading** - Emotions, supernatural nature, lies
- **Magic detection** - See spells, rituals, enchantments
- **True sight** - Pierce illusions and disguises
- **Complete awareness** - Supernatural and mundane simultaneously

### Limitations

- **Perception only** - Seeing doesn't mean affecting
- **Information overload** - Constant supernatural input (high Desperation)
- **Conspicuous** - Others notice you seeing "nothing"
- **Sanity strain** - Witnessing horror constantly
- **Storyteller-dependent** - Only useful if supernatural is present

## All Perks

### Spirit Sight

**What It Does:**
See ghosts, spirits, and incorporeal entities as clearly as physical beings. The invisible world is visible to you.

**What You See:**
- **Ghosts** - Spirits of the dead
- **Nature spirits** - Elemental/location spirits
- **Demons** - Incorporeal entities (when not possessing)
- **Astral projections** - Out-of-body travelers
- **Incorporeal supernatural** - Anything non-physical

**When To Use:**
- **Ghost investigations** - See haunting spirits
- **Spirit communication** - Visual confirmation
- **Incorporeal threats** - Detect invisible enemies
- **Haunted locations** - Identify all spirits present
- **Death investigations** - Speak with murder victims' ghosts

**Strategy Notes:**
- Always active (passive perception)
- Synergizes with Occult skill (understand what you see)
- Can be overwhelming (many ghosts everywhere)
- Essential for spirit-focused chronicles

**Example:**
```
Investigating murder scene
Spirit Sight: You see ghost of victim standing over their body
- Victim's ghost: Visible, translucent, bloodied
- Ghost gestures toward door (killer exited there)
- Ghost points at their eyes, then at mirror
  (they saw killer's reflection)

You address ghost directly (others see you talking to empty air):
"Can you describe what you saw?"
Ghost communicates (gestures, writing in spectral blood)
Investigation aided by eyewitness testimony from victim
Evidence others can't access
```

### Aura Reading

**What It Does:**
See auras surrounding living beings - colored energy fields revealing emotions, supernatural nature, health, and lies.

**What Auras Reveal:**
- **Emotions** - Current emotional state (fear, anger, love, etc.)
- **Supernatural nature** - Vampire, werewolf, mage, possessed, etc.
- **Health status** - Wounded, diseased, dying
- **Lies** - Aura flickers when lying
- **Intent** - Hostile, friendly, neutral
- **Desperation level** - See others' Desperation (glowing intensity)

**Mechanical Effect:**
- Action: Look at person, see aura (reflexive, no cost)
- Automatic knowledge: Storyteller describes aura
- Lie detection: Add Desperation to Insight rolls to detect deception
- Supernatural identification: Instantly recognize supernatural beings

**When To Use:**
- **Interrogation** - Detect lies automatically
- **Supernatural identification** - Know vampire from human instantly
- **Threat assessment** - See hostile intent
- **Social scenes** - Read emotional states
- **Investigation** - Identify suspects by aura

**Strategy Notes:**
- Reflexive (always active, no action cost)
- Synergizes with Insight skill (understand emotions)
- Social force multiplier (impossible to lie to you)
- Combat intelligence (see which enemies are supernatural)

**Example:**
```
Interviewing suspect in vampire murder
Aura Reading: You see their aura
- Color: Pale blue-white (undead, vampire)
- Emotional state: Anxiety, fear (they're scared)
- Lies: Aura flickers when they say "I wasn't there"
- Desperation: 0 (vampire, doesn't track Desperation)

You: "You're lying. You were there. And you're a vampire."
Suspect (shocked): "How did you...?"
You: "Your aura tells me everything."

Insight roll to press advantage:
Manipulation 3 + Insight 4 + Desperation 3 (Aura Reading lie detection) = 10 dice
/roll pool:10 difficulty:2 comment:Pressing vampire suspect
Result: 6 successes - vampire breaks, confesses
Investigation solved through supernatural perception
```

**Herald Commands:**
```
/perks edge:Third Eye perk:Aura Reading
```

### See Magic

**What It Does:**
Perceive active magic, enchantments, rituals, and supernatural effects. Magic is visible as energy patterns, glowing symbols, or crackling power.

**What You See:**
- **Active spells** - Magic being cast appears visually
- **Enchantments** - Magical items glow with power
- **Rituals** - See ritual energy building/flowing
- **Wards** - Protective barriers visible
- **Curses** - See curse energy on victims
- **Supernatural effects** - Vampire powers, demon abilities, etc.

**When To Use:**
- **Ritual detection** - See magic being performed
- **Item identification** - Detect magical artifacts
- **Curse diagnosis** - See supernatural afflictions
- **Threat detection** - Perceive incoming spells
- **Investigation** - Magical evidence gathering

**Strategy Notes:**
- Always active (passive perception)
- Combat utility (see powers being used)
- Investigation focus (magical evidence)
- Synergizes with Occult skill (understand magic seen)

**Example:**
```
Entering abandoned church, cult suspected
See Magic: You perceive supernatural energy
- Floor: Ritual circle glowing with crimson power (active ritual in progress)
- Altar: Enchanted dagger pulsing with dark energy (artifact)
- Air: Threads of magic flowing toward back room (ritual focus there)
- Victim: Curse energy coiled around their aura (hexed)

You relay to team:
"Active ritual in back room, they're channeling power through victim.
Enchanted dagger on altar - don't touch it.
Ritual circle on floor - if we disrupt it, ritual fails."

Tactical intelligence through magical perception
Team disrupts ritual before completion
```

### True Sight

**What It Does:**
See through supernatural illusions, disguises, and deceptions. Supernatural beings appear in their true forms regardless of powers used to hide.

**What True Sight Pierces:**
- **Shapeshifting** - See werewolf despite human form
- **Vampire disguise** - See corpse beneath beautiful mask
- **Demon possession** - See demon within host
- **Illusions** - See reality behind magical deceptions
- **Invisibility** - See invisible entities
- **Mind control** - See person is dominated/controlled

**Mechanical Effect:**
- Automatic: Supernatural disguises fail against you
- Roll (if contested): Resolve + Occult + Desperation vs supernatural power
- Success: See true form/reality
- Always active (passive)

**When To Use:**
- **Supernatural identification** - Know what creature really is
- **Infiltration detection** - See shapeshifter infiltrators
- **Possession diagnosis** - See demon riding host
- **Illusion negation** - Unaffected by deceptions
- **Stealth detection** - See invisible enemies

**Strategy Notes:**
- Passive (always on)
- Counter to supernatural stealth/disguise
- May be contested (strong illusions vs your sight)
- Narrative impact (you see horror others don't)

**Example:**
```
Meeting with "human" contact in restaurant
True Sight: You see their true form
- Appears human to others
- You see: Pale skin, no breath, predatory aura, fangs
- Recognition: Ancient vampire in human guise

You (to team via earpiece): "Contact is vampire, ancient one.
Everyone stay calm, hands near weapons."

Later, vampire uses illusion power to appear as ally
Others fooled, you unaffected
True Sight: You still see vampire
"Nice try. I see what you really are."
Immune to supernatural deception
```

**Herald Commands:**
```
/perks edge:Third Eye perk:True Sight
```

### Mystic Vision

**What It Does:**
Ultimate supernatural perception - combine all Third Eye abilities and extend them through time. See past events (psychometry), glimpse possible futures, and perceive supernatural connections.

**Enhanced Capabilities:**
- **Psychometry** - Touch object, see its history (visions of past)
- **Future glimpses** - Brief flashes of possible futures
- **Connection sight** - See supernatural bonds (sire-childe, familiar bonds, etc.)
- **Ley lines** - Perceive flows of mystical energy
- **True names** - See a being's true nature and name
- **Complete perception** - All Third Eye abilities enhanced

**Mechanical Effect:**
- Psychometry: Touch object, roll Wits + Occult + Desperation, visions of past
- Future glimpses: Storyteller provides brief precognitive flashes
- Connection sight: Automatically see supernatural relationships
- Passive enhancement to all Third Eye perks

**When To Use:**
- **Investigation** - Psychometry on evidence
- **Planning** - Future glimpses for strategy
- **Relationship mapping** - See vampire family trees
- **True names** - Power over supernatural beings
- **Ultimate perception** - Know everything visible and invisible

**Strategy Notes:**
- Most powerful Third Eye perk
- Combines all previous abilities
- Adds temporal perception (past/future)
- Psychometry excellent for investigations

**Example:**

```
Crime scene: Victim killed by unknown supernatural
Mystic Vision: Multiple perception modes

- Vampire face clear in vision

Connection sight: See vampire's sire bond (connected to vampire elder in city)
Future glimpse: Flash of vampire returning tonight (precognition)

Complete intelligence from supernatural perception:
- Killer identity: Marcus (vampire)
- Connection: Childe of vampire elder
- Location: Old theater
- Pattern: Will return tonight
Perfect case solved through Mystic Vision
```

## Strategy Guide

### Early Game (Characters 0-20 XP)

**Starting with Third Eye:**
- **Focus:** Basic supernatural perception
- **Best Perks:** Spirit Sight, Aura Reading
- **Build:** High Wits 3-4, Occult 3-4, Awareness 3+
- **Role:** Supernatural investigator

**Recommended Build:**
- Wits 4, Resolve 3 (perceive and understand)
- Occult 4, Awareness 3, Investigation 3
- Spirit Sight first (see invisible)
- Investigation/social role

**Investigation Pattern:**
- Spirit Sight for ghost evidence
- Aura Reading for lie detection and supernatural ID
- Standard investigation skills + supernatural perception
- Relay information to team

### Mid Game (Characters 21-50 XP)

**Developing Third Eye:**
- **Add Perks:** See Magic, True Sight
- **Expand Role:** Complete supernatural perception
- **Occult Expertise:** Understand what you perceive
- **Team Integration:** Supernatural intelligence specialist

**Advanced Tactics:**
- Spirit Sight for ghosts/spirits
- Aura Reading for social/interrogation
- See Magic for ritual/curse detection
- True Sight to pierce deceptions
- Complete supernatural awareness

**Progression:**
- Raise Occult to 5 (understand all perceptions)
- Raise Awareness to 4-5 (notice everything)
- Raise Investigation to 4 (analyze perceptions)
- Second Edge: Global Access (mundane + supernatural intelligence), Sense Danger (physical + mystical awareness)

### Late Game (Characters 51+ XP)

**Mastering Third Eye:**
- **All Perks:** Including Mystic Vision
- **Psychometry:** See past through objects
- **Future glimpses:** Temporal perception
- **Complete sight:** Nothing hidden from you

**End-game Tactics:**
- All Third Eye perks active simultaneously
- Mystic Vision for psychometry and future sight
- See everything: past, present, future, visible, invisible
- Complete supernatural awareness
- Nearly omniscient within supernatural domains

**Ultimate Build:**
- Wits 5, Intelligence 4, Resolve 3
- Occult 5, Awareness 5, Investigation 5
- All Third Eye perks
- Second/Third Edge: Global Access, Sense Danger, Library
- Supernatural information master

### Edge Synergies

**Works Great With:**
- **Innocent Creed** - Investigator with supernatural sight
- **Global Access Edge** - Mundane + supernatural intelligence = total knowledge
- **Sense Danger Edge** - Precognition + mystic sight = complete awareness
- **Library Edge** - Research + perception = understanding
- **High Occult** - Understand what you see
- **High Wits/Awareness** - Notice supernatural details

**Conflicts With:**
- **Combat builds** - Third Eye is perception, not action
- **Mundane concepts** - Seeing supernatural constantly is intense

**Neutral:**
- **Arsenal, Fleet, Contacts** - Different focus but compatible

### Using Third Eye in Play

Third Eye operates through supernatural perception:

1. **Perception active** - Third Eye perks are always on (passive)
2. **Supernatural present** - You automatically perceive it
3. **Describe what you see** - Player describes mystic perception
4. **Storyteller confirms** - GM provides details of what's perceived
5. **Act on knowledge** - Use supernatural intelligence

**Example:**
```
Entering haunted mansion
Third Eye activated (passive perks):

Spirit Sight: You see 7 ghosts throughout mansion
- 3 in foyer (family members, murdered)
- 2 upstairs (children, crying)
- 1 in basement (chained, tortured)
- 1 outside (groundskeeper, watching)

Aura Reading: Living person detected upstairs (aura shows fear, human)
See Magic: Ritual circle in basement glowing with active power
True Sight: "Empty" mirror actually contains trapped spirit

You relay to team:
"Seven ghosts total - three here, two upstairs, one in basement, one outside.
There's a living person upstairs, terrified.
Active ritual in basement - someone's channeling the chained ghost's power.
Don't trust the mirror - trapped spirit inside."

Team operates with perfect supernatural intelligence
Investigation solved before entering
Supernatural perception changes everything
```
