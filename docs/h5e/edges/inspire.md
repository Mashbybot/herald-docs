Inspire represents a Hunter's ability to bolster, motivate, and empower allies through supernatural charisma and leadership. Your presence makes others braver, stronger, and more capable. You're the force multiplier.

## Overview

### What is Inspire?

Inspire is an **Aptitude Edge** that grants supernatural leadership and ally enhancement. When you invoke Inspire, your allies fight harder, resist fear, and push beyond their limits. Your words and presence supernaturally empower those who fight alongside you.

The Edge operates through **supernatural charisma**: you channel supernatural energy through leadership, making your allies superhuman when they need it most. This isn't just motivation - it's mystical empowerment.

### Who Should Take This Edge?

**Ideal For:**
- **Leaders** - Cell commanders
- **Support characters** - Make allies stronger
- **Defenders** - Protect through empowerment
- **Charismatic builds** - High Charisma/Leadership
- **Team players** - Enhance group effectiveness

**Consider Carefully If:**
- You're a solo Hunter (no allies to inspire)
- You have low Charisma
- You prefer selfish tactics
- Your group is small (1-2 people)

### Key Strengths

- **Force multiplier** - Makes entire team stronger
- **Team survival** - Buff ally defenses and rolls
- **Morale** - Resist fear and despair effects
- **Leadership** - Command effectively in chaos
- **Synergy** - Best in groups

### Limitations

- **Requires allies** - Useless if solo
- **Action economy** - Buffing allies costs your actions
- **Range limited** - Must be near allies
- **Desperation cost** - Using Inspire increases Desperation
- **Indirect power** - You're strong through others

## All Perks

### Rally

**What It Does:**
Spend an action to grant all nearby allies bonus dice equal to your Desperation rating on their next action.

**Mechanical Effect:**
- Action: Rally allies (replaces your attack/action)
- Range: Line of sight / hearing distance
- Effect: Allies add your Desperation to next roll
- Duration: One action per ally

**When To Use:**
- **Combat start** - Buff team before engagement
- **Critical moments** - Empower allies for key rolls
- **Desperate situations** - Turn the tide
- **Coordinated strikes** - Everyone attacks buffed

**Strategy Notes:**
- Best with high Desperation (more buff)
- Sacrifice your action for team power
- Coordinate with allies for simultaneous buffed attacks
- Great for bossfights

**Example:**
```
Fighting vampire elder with 3 allies
Use Rally: All 3 allies gain +3 dice (your Desperation 3)
Ally 1: 6 dice + 3 = 9 dice attack
Ally 2: 7 dice + 3 = 10 dice attack
Ally 3: 5 dice + 3 = 8 dice attack
Team overwhelms vampire with coordinated buffed strikes
```

**Herald Commands:**
```
/edge edge:Inspire
/perks edge:Inspire perk:Rally
```

### Fearless

**What It Does:**
Your allies are immune to supernatural fear and intimidation while you're present and conscious. Your presence banishes terror.

**Mechanical Effect:**
- Automatic effect (always on when you're conscious)
- Allies immune to fear effects
- Allies immune to supernatural intimidation
- Ignore morale breaks

**When To Use:**
- **Terror enemies** - Vampires, demons, eldritch horrors
- **Intimidation tactics** - Enemy psychological warfare
- **Morale crisis** - Keep team fighting
- **Horror situations** - Resist mind-breaking revelations

**Strategy Notes:**
- Passive benefit (no action needed)
- Invaluable against fear-using enemies
- Keep yourself conscious (if you fall, allies lose immunity)
- Makes normal humans fight supernatural horrors

**Example:**
```
Demon appears, using supernatural terror aura
Normal: Allies must resist or flee
Fearless: Your presence negates fear
Allies fight on despite demon's horror
Team maintains cohesion and effectiveness
```

**Herald Commands:**
```
/perks edge:Inspire perk:Fearless
```

### Empower

**What It Does:**
Touch an ally and transfer Willpower points to them, restoring their reserves.

**Mechanical Effect:**
- Action: Touch ally
- Transfer: Give X Willpower from your pool to theirs
- Limit: Can't exceed their maximum
- Cost: Your Willpower reduced by amount given

**When To Use:**
- **Ally depleted** - They're out of Willpower
- **Critical moment** - They need Willpower for key action
- **Resource management** - Redistribute Willpower optimally
- **Support role** - You have Willpower to spare

**Strategy Notes:**
- Best with high Willpower pool (Composure + Resolve)
- Sacrifice your resources for ally capability
- Coordinate with ally on when they need it
- Build for high Willpower if using this regularly

**Example:**
```
Ally needs to resist vampire mesmerism (Willpower cost)
They're at 0 Willpower
You have 5 Willpower remaining
Empower: Transfer 2 Willpower to them
They resist mesmerism successfully
You're at 3 Willpower, they're at 2
```

**Herald Commands:**
```
/perks edge:Inspire perk:Empower
```

### Command Presence

**What It Does:**
Your orders are obeyed instantly and without question. Allies execute your commands with supernatural precision and coordination.

**Mechanical Effect:**
- Leadership rolls gain advantage
- Allies gain bonus to rolls when following your orders
- Coordinated actions simplified
- Tactical commands automatically understood

**When To Use:**
- **Tactical coordination** - Complex team maneuvers
- **Crisis leadership** - Chaos requires clear command
- **Team tactics** - Synchronized actions
- **NPC leadership** - Command NPCs effectively

**Strategy Notes:**
- Requires Leadership skill
- Best with high Charisma
- Enables complex tactics
- Great for Storytellers who enjoy tactical play

**Example:**
```
Fighting multiple vampires, need coordinated assault
Command Presence: Issue tactical orders
"Alpha, flank left! Bravo, suppressing fire! Charlie, grenade!"
All allies gain bonus from clear command structure
Execute coordinated strike with supernatural precision
Vampires overwhelmed by organized assault
```

**Herald Commands:**
```
/perks edge:Inspire perk:Command Presence
```

### Martyr's Cry

**What It Does:**
When you take damage, all allies within range gain temporary bonus dice equal to the damage you took on their next action.

**Mechanical Effect:**
- Trigger: You take damage
- Effect: Allies gain bonus = damage amount
- Duration: Next action per ally
- Range: Line of sight

**When To Use:**
- **Tank role** - Take hits intentionally to buff allies
- **Desperate moments** - Your sacrifice empowers team
- **Boss fights** - Convert damage to team offense
- **Inspiration** - Seeing you hurt enrages allies

**Strategy Notes:**
- Synergizes with high Health/Stamina (survive more damage)
- Intentionally take hits to buff team
- Pairs with Martyr Edge (similar theme)
- Narrative power (sacrifice inspires others)

**Example:**
```
Vampire elder hits you for 4 lethal damage
Martyr's Cry activates
All 3 allies gain +4 dice on next action
Ally 1: Attacks with 11 dice (normally 7)
Ally 2: Attacks with 10 dice (normally 6)
Ally 3: Attacks with 12 dice (normally 8)
Your sacrifice turns the battle
Team destroys vampire while you heal
```

**Herald Commands:**
```
/perks edge:Inspire perk:Martyr's Cry
```

## Strategy Guide

### Early Game (Characters 0-20 XP)

**Starting with Inspire:**
- **Focus:** Basic ally buffing
- **Best Perks:** Rally, Fearless
- **Build:** Charisma 4, Leadership 3-4, Willpower 5+
- **Tactics:** Buff allies, maintain presence

**Recommended Build:**
- Charisma 4, Composure 3, Resolve 2 (Willpower 5)
- Leadership 4, Persuasion 3
- Firearms 2-3 (still contribute to combat)
- Rally first, Fearless second

**Support Pattern:**
- Start combat with Rally
- Position centrally (maintain Fearless radius)
- Issue commands (Command Presence if taken)
- Attack when allies don't need buffs

### Mid Game (Characters 21-50 XP)

**Developing Inspire:**
- **Add Perks:** Empower, Command Presence
- **Expand Role:** Tactical commander + support
- **Build Willpower:** Increase Composure/Resolve
- **Team Synergy:** Coordinate with allies

**Advanced Tactics:**
- Rally before key moments
- Empower depleted allies
- Command Presence for coordinated strikes
- Maintain Fearless presence

**Progression:**
- Raise Charisma to 5 (maximum leadership)
- Raise Leadership to 5 (best commands)
- Raise Composure/Resolve (more Willpower to share)
- Second Edge: Arsenal (arm the team), Status (institutional leadership)

### Late Game (Characters 51+ XP)

**Mastering Inspire:**
- **All Perks:** Complete support mastery
- **Team Lynchpin:** Essential to group
- **Force Multiplier:** Make allies superhuman
- **Tactical Genius:** Perfect coordination

**End-game Tactics:**
- Rally + Command Presence combinations
- Strategic Empower use
- Martyr's Cry for dramatic turns
- Fearless against horrific enemies

**Ultimate Build:**
- Charisma 5, Composure 4, Resolve 3 (Willpower 7)
- Leadership 5, Persuasion 5
- All Inspire perks
- Second/Third Edge: Arsenal, Status, or Benediction
- Team commander and buffer

### Edge Synergies

**Works Great With:**
- **Defender Creed** - Protect through empowerment
- **Zealot Creed** - Lead the crusade
- **Arsenal Edge** - Equip and inspire team
- **Status Edge** - Official leadership + supernatural inspiration
- **Benediction Edge** - Divine leader healing and inspiring
- **High Charisma** - Maximum inspiration effect

**Conflicts With:**
- **Solo play** - No allies to inspire
- **Selfish builds** - Inspire is team-focused
- **Low Charisma** - Less effective leadership

**Neutral:**
- **Library, Contacts, Fleet** - Different focus but compatible

### Using Inspire in Play

Inspire operates through action and presence:

1. **Rally** - Declare Rally action, allies gain bonus
2. **Fearless** - Automatic (always active when conscious)
3. **Empower** - Touch ally, transfer Willpower
4. **Command Presence** - Issue orders, allies execute perfectly
5. **Martyr's Cry** - Automatic when you take damage

**Example:**
```
Combat begins: Vampire coterie (4 vampires) vs your cell (you + 3 allies)
Turn 1: You use Rally
Storyteller: "Your allies gain +3 dice (your Desperation 3) on next action"
Allies attack with buffed pools:
Ally 1: /roll pool:10 difficulty:2 comment:Buffed attack (+3 from Rally)
Ally 2: /roll pool:9 difficulty:2 comment:Buffed attack (+3 from Rally)
Ally 3: /roll pool:11 difficulty:2 comment:Buffed attack (+3 from Rally)
Coordinated buffed assault devastates vampires
```
