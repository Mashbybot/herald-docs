## Overview

### What is Fleet?

Fleet is an **Aptitude Edge** that grants superhuman speed and agility. You run faster than humanly possible, react with lightning reflexes, and perform acrobatic feats that defy physics. This isn't training - it's supernatural enhancement.

The Edge operates through **supernatural enhancement**: when you invoke Fleet, you become impossibly fast. Time seems to slow for you as you blur past enemies, dodge bullets, and cover impossible distances in seconds.

### Who Should Take This Edge?

**Ideal For:**
- **Combat specialists** - Mobile hit-and-run tactics
- **Scouts** - Fast reconnaissance
- **Defenders** - Rapid response to threats
- **Escape artists** - Never trapped
- **Pursuit specialists** - Chase down any target

**Consider Carefully If:**
- Your chronicle is confined/indoor focused (limited room to run)
- You prefer stationary tactics
- You don't have Athletics skill
- Your Dexterity is low (Fleet works best with high Dex)

### Key Strengths

- **Combat mobility** - Superior positioning in fights
- **Escape capability** - Run from anything
- **Pursuit** - Chase down fleeing enemies
- **Initiative advantage** - Act first consistently
- **Acrobatic feats** - Impossible maneuvers

### Limitations

- **Requires space** - Limited in tight confines
- **Desperation cost** - Using Fleet increases Desperation
- **Obvious supernatural** - Hard to hide this ability
- **Stamina drain** - Can't maintain forever
- **May separate from team** - Too fast for allies

## All Perks

### Lightning Reflexes

**What It Does:**
You act first in combat, outpacing even the fastest opponents. Add your Desperation rating to initiative rolls.

**Mechanical Effect:**
- Initiative = Wits + Dexterity + Desperation
- Always act before normal opponents
- Reroll initiative ties (you win)

**When To Use:**
- **Combat start** - Strike first
- **Ambush situations** - React before ambushers
- **Time-critical moments** - Act before disaster
- **Chase initiation** - Start pursuit immediately

**Strategy Notes:**
- Synergizes with high Wits and Dexterity
- First strike often decisive
- Pairs with Alpha Strike tactics
- Essential for assassination builds

**Example:**
```
Combat starts: Vampire ambush
Normal initiative: Wits 3 + Dex 4 = 7 dice
Fleet + Lightning Reflexes: 7 + Desperation 3 = 10 dice
You roll 6 successes vs vampire's 3
You act first - shoot vampire before it reaches you
```

**Herald Commands:**
```
/edge edge:Fleet
/perks edge:Fleet perk:Lightning Reflexes
```

### Blur

**What It Does:**
Move so fast you're hard to hit. Add your Desperation rating to defense rolls (dodging, evasion).

**Mechanical Effect:**
- Defense rolls = Base pool + Desperation
- Applies to dodge, evasion, avoiding attacks
- Visual effect: You blur and streak

**When To Use:**
- **Under attack** - Dodge supernaturally
- **Overwhelmed** - Evade multiple attackers
- **Bullet dodging** - Supernatural reflexes
- **Escape** - Slip past enemies

**Strategy Notes:**
- Makes you very hard to hit
- Essential for survival in combat
- Pairs with Athletics skill
- Higher Desperation = better defense

### Momentum Strike

**What It Does:**
Channel your speed into devastating attacks. Add successes from Sprint/movement rolls as bonus damage to your next attack.

**Mechanical Effect:**
- Sprint/move at high speed
- Margin of success becomes bonus damage
- One attack after movement
- Kinetic energy = damage

**When To Use:**
- **Charge attacks** - Sprint then strike
- **Hit-and-run** - Maximum damage, then escape
- **Assassination** - Speed strike for massive damage
- **Alpha strikes** - First hit decisive

**Strategy Notes:**
- Combines mobility and offense
- Devastating opening attacks
- Requires setup (Sprint first)
- Best with high Athletics/combat skills

**Example:**
```
Sprint across battlefield (Fleet active)
Athletics roll: 8 successes, margin 5
Crash into vampire with supernatural speed
Attack: Normal damage + 5 bonus from momentum
Devastating hit, vampire severely wounded
Continue with follow-up attacks
```

```
/perks edge:Fleet perk:Momentum Strike
```

## Strategy Guide

### Early Game (Characters 0-20 XP)

**Starting with Fleet:**
- **Focus:** Mobility and defense
- **Best Perks:** Lightning Reflexes, Blur
- **Build:** High Dexterity (4), Athletics (3-4)
- **Tactics:** Act first, dodge everything, mobile combat

**Recommended Build:**
- Dexterity 4, Stamina 3, Wits 3
- Firearms 4 or Melee 4, Athletics 4
- Lightning Reflexes first (initiative)
- Blur second (survival)

**Combat Pattern:**
- Act first (Lightning Reflexes)
- Attack with advantage (first strike)
- Dodge counters (Blur)
- Reposition (move fast)

### Mid Game (Characters 21-50 XP)

**Developing Fleet:**
- **Add Perks:** Sprint, Parkour
- **Expand Tactics:** Hit-and-run, pursuit, escape
- **Synergy:** Firearms for mobile shooting, Melee for speed strikes
- **Team Role:** Scout, flanker, pursuer

**Advanced Tactics:**
- Sprint to optimal positions
- Parkour for vertical advantage
- Blur when focused
- Lightning Reflexes always

**Progression:**
- Raise Athletics to 5 (maximum mobility)
- Raise combat skill to 5 (capitalize on positioning)
- Second Edge: Arsenal (mobile weapons platform), Senses (awareness + speed)

### Late Game (Characters 51+ XP)

**Mastering Fleet:**
- **All Perks:** Complete speed mastery
- **Untouchable:** Nearly impossible to hit
- **Everywhere:** Control entire battlefield
- **Devastating:** Momentum strikes obliterate enemies

**End-game Tactics:**
- Sprint + Momentum Strike alpha strikes
- Parkour + Stealth infiltration
- Blur + Dodge tank (absorb aggro, dodge everything)
- Lightning Reflexes + team coordination (set up allies)

**Ultimate Build:**
- Dexterity 5, Athletics 5, Firearms/Melee 5
- All Fleet perks
- Second/Third Edge: Arsenal, Senses, or Inspire
- Fastest Hunter alive

### Edge Synergies

**Works Great With:**
- **Arsenal Edge** - Mobile weapons platform
- **Senses Edge** - Awareness + speed = perfect scout
- **Avenger Creed** - Fast vengeance
- **Defender Creed** - Rapid response protector
- **High Dexterity** - Maximize speed advantage

**Conflicts With:**
- **Stationary tactics** - Wasted on defensive positions
- **Low Athletics** - Can't fully utilize
- **Tight environments** - No room to run

**Neutral:**
- **Library, Contacts, Status** - Different focus but compatible

### Using Fleet in Play

Fleet operates through Desperation-fueled enhancement:

1. **Invoke Fleet** - Declare you're using supernatural speed
2. **Add Desperation** - Bonuses = your Desperation rating
3. **Describe effect** - Blur, speed trails, impossible movements
4. **Resolve action** - Roll with bonuses
5. **Desperation consequences** - Using Fleet may increase Desperation

**Example:**
```
Storyteller: "Three vampires surround you. Roll initiative."
You: "I invoke Fleet - Lightning Reflexes. My initiative is Wits 4 + Dex 5 + Desperation 3 = 12 dice"
/roll pool:12 difficulty:0 comment:Fleet-enhanced initiative
Result: 7 successes (vampires get 3, 4, 4)
You act first, targeting the lead vampire before they can attack
```
